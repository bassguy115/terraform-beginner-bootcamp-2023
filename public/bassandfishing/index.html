Fishing for Bass and Fishing in general is a great challenge!!

Bass fishing is a popular and exciting form of angling. It involves pursuing various species of bass, such as largemouth bass and smallmouth bass, found in both freshwater lakes and rivers. Anglers use a variety of lures and techniques to catch these elusive and powerful fish. Whether you're a seasoned angler or a novice, bass fishing offers a thrilling and rewarding experience in the world of fishing. These are all Smallmouth Bass from the Susquehanna River in Maryland.
